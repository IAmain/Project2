Welcome to Saving Gary!

Little Gary was walking along the shore when he accidentally spilled all his gold-star stickers!
Gary wants to collect as many of them back as possible, but there's just one problem...
There are sharks all over, and Gary's just a small turtle!
Help Gary swim as far as he can, collecting his star stickers along the way, and avoid the sharks!

Directions:
-Movement: Use the left and right arrow keys to move
-Objective: Collect stars and avoid sharks!
-You have three lives - each time you hit a shark, one life will be lost.
-Your score increments automatically depending on how long you've kept Gary alive
-Each star you collect will add 10 points to your score!
-Be careful not to get hit three times or else it's Game Over!
